Hospital Management System Information
Our Hospital Management System (HMS) was established in 2010 to revolutionize healthcare management. It was created in response to the growing need for efficient hospital operations in urban areas. The system was developed in New York City by a team of healthcare professionals and IT experts.

The HMS manages all aspects of hospital operations, from patient records and appointment scheduling to billing and inventory management. Currently, our hospital employs 150 doctors and serves 2000 patients.
